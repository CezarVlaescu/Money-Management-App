🔐

Local-first budgeting

Money Bloom stores your income, expenses and savings goals locally in your browser. The current version does not upload your personal budget data to a server.

📱

Stored on this device

Your data is saved in your browser storage. If you clear browser data or use another device, your Money Bloom data may not be available there.

🏦

No bank connection yet

The current version does not connect to your bank account and does not read real bank transactions. Transactions are added manually by you.

👤

No account required

You do not need to create an account to use the app. Your budget can be managed directly on your phone.

🌱

Future integrations

If bank sync or AI advice is added later, the app should clearly explain what data is used before enabling those features.

Important note

Money Bloom is a budgeting helper, not financial advice. You are responsible for your own spending, saving and investment decisions.
